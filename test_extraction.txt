BLUEBERRIES (Heidelbeeren)
==========================
Category: Fruit
Serving size: 1 cup (148g)

Nutritional Profile per 100g:
- Carbohydrates: 14.5g
- Fiber: 2.4g
- Protein: 0.7g
- Vitamin C: 9.7mg
- Vitamin K: 19.3mcg
- Manganese: 0.34mg

Health Benefits:
Blueberries are rich in anthocyanins, powerful antioxidants that give them their blue color.
Studies show regular consumption may improve memory and cognitive function in older adults.
The polyphenol content (estimated 550mg per 100g) supports cardiovascular health.

WALNUTS (Walnüsse)
==================
Category: Nuts & seeds
Serving size: 1 oz (28g)

Nutritional Profile per 100g:
- Protein: 15.2g
- Fat: 65.2g
- Carbohydrates: 13.7g
- Fiber: 6.7g
- Omega-3 (ALA): 9.1g
- Magnesium: 158mg
- Phosphorus: 346mg
- Vitamin E: 0.7mg

Health Benefits:
Walnuts contain the highest amount of omega-3 fatty acids among nuts.
Research links walnut consumption to improved brain health and reduced inflammation.
The combination of healthy fats, fiber, and antioxidants supports heart health.

TURMERIC (Kurkuma)
==================
Category: Herbs & spices
Serving size: 1 teaspoon (3g)

Nutritional Profile per 100g:
- Carbohydrates: 64.9g
- Fiber: 21g
- Protein: 7.8g
- Iron: 41.4mg
- Manganese: 7.8mg
- Curcumin (active compound): ~3g

Health Benefits:
Turmeric contains curcumin, a compound with powerful anti-inflammatory properties.
Studies suggest it may help with joint pain, digestive issues, and cognitive function.
Absorption is greatly enhanced when consumed with black pepper (piperine).
